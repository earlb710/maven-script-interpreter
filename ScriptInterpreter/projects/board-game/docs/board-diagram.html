4-Quadrant Monopoly-Style Board Game

A large-scale board game with 4 quadrants sharing borders at the center.
Each quadrant contains 40 spaces (like a standard Monopoly board).

Legend

  • Quadrant 1 (NW) - Luxury District: High-value properties ($200-$400)
  • Quadrant 2 (NE) - Commercial District: Business properties ($150-$300)
  • Quadrant 3 (SW) - Residential District: Medium properties ($100-$200)
  • Quadrant 4 (SE) - Industrial District: Starter properties ($60-$150)
  • CENTER START: All players begin here and choose a direction
  • Red Lines: Shared borders between adjacent quadrants
  • Dashed Borders: Spaces on shared borders (counted once)

Board Statistics

  • Total Quadrants: 4 (Q1-NW, Q2-NE, Q3-SW, Q4-SE)
  • Spaces per Quadrant (view): 10 per side (some sides shared with adjacent quadrants)
  • Unique Cells per Quadrant: 19 cells (10 on one side + 9 on adjacent, 1 corner overlaps)
  • Shared Cells per Quadrant: 17 cells (9 + 8, accounting for corners)
  • Shared Cross Structure: 33 cells (8+8+8+8+1 center)
  • Total Unique Spaces: 109 (33 shared cross + 76 unique quadrant cells)
  • Shared Borders: Q1↔Q2, Q1↔Q3, Q2↔Q4, Q3↔Q4

Gameplay Notes

  • Players start at the CENTER START and choose one of 4 directions
  • Move clockwise within your current quadrant (like standard Monopoly)
  • When landing on a shared border space, you can cross into the adjacent quadrant
  • Collect $200 when passing through CENTER START
  • Strategic gameplay: Choose quadrants based on property availability and opponents' positions
